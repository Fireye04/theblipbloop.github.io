A Website

23 November 2024

I finally made a website! I’ve been meaning to do so for years and I finally got around to it. I think this will be a nice place to act as a portfolio for some of the projects I’ve worked on.

I created this site from scratch using Hugo while stuck home sick with COVID. It’s written entirely in HTML, CSS, and Markdown. This is my first expedition into web development and it’s been quite fun.

The site still needs a lot of work! That said, I’m happy with how its coming along.
